This is a great way to get on the explore feed, as IG's systems will detect that you're receiving a large number of views shortly after being uploaded.

3. Get more views by linking your other social media accounts such as Facebook, Twitter and Youtube. This is an effective and easy way to gain more views and converting those viewers into followers.
